Our Mission

LVRT (Love Art) runs the entire business and legal backend of the Entertainment Industry with software and Internet services. LVRT is a 3-sided platform with a specific mission and execution strategy for each of 3 customer groups: Artists, Consumers, and Advertisers.
Sunday Session provides a platform to upcoming artists and brings them together.

Customer Groups

1

Artists

LVRT's mission for Artists is to help them get discovered and ensure they are fairly paid for their creative works. This is accomplished through a suite of tools like Quickbooks, LegalZoom and Twitter rolled into one and focused on Artists' needs.

2

Consumers

LVRT's mission for Consumers is to give them access to quality content, when and where they want, with simple yet powerful user interfaces and convenient payment methods. This is accomplished through a distribution service like Spotify, Netflix and iTunes combined.

3

Advertisers

LVRT's mission for Advertisers is to help them reach their target demographics with meaningful marketing messages. This is accomplished through an ad targeting system and tools for product placement.
